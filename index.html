Data sovereignty & privacy

How your data is stored

Your ledger is saved in IndexedDB and encrypted with AES-256-GCM before it touches disk. The key is created on this device, marked non-extractable, and stored locally — inspecting storage shows only ciphertext.

Autosave writes every change to encrypted local storage automatically. The disk button in the header pauses it for a session where changes stay in memory only.

Encryption requires a secure context (https:// or localhost). The status chip on the calendar shows whether encryption and autosave are active.

Backups & moving between devices

1
Export

Download an encrypted .zip with ledger.enc.json and ledger.key.json.

2
Split for safety

Store the key separately from the encrypted file — neither is useful alone.

3
Import

Load the .zip or the two files in either order on any device. Legacy plaintext .json still imports.

Technical structure

Calendar dates are unique keys mapping to expense objects — the same shape used in exports and inside the encrypted record.

{
  "name": "GBA Expenses",
  "events": {
    "2026-06-03": [
      { "title": "API Hosting", "price": 49.99, "recurring": true, "paid": false }
    ]
  }
}

Security notes

  • Device-bound keys protect against casual inspection, not full browser access.
  • Backup often — export your encrypted .zip periodically.
  • Split sensitive sets — key and ciphertext apart when storing or sending.
  • No syncing — move exports manually between computers.

Intelligent assistance

AI Operator

OpenExpense includes local, privacy-respecting AI tools to speed up expense entry — without sending your receipts or ledger to the cloud.

Receipt scanning

Click Scan Receipt to photograph or upload a receipt. OCR extracts merchant, date, and amount for you to review before saving.

Runs in your browser

Models download once (~5 MB) and cache locally. Processing happens on your device — not on our servers, because there aren't any.

Human-in-the-loop by default

Every scanned field is shown in a review sheet before it touches your ledger. You confirm or edit title, date, amount, and notes — nothing is auto-saved without your approval.

  • OCR suggests values; you decide what gets saved.
  • Raw scanned text is available if you need to verify parsing.
  • Works offline after the first model download.
